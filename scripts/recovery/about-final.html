About Sanadana

Reviving the Science of Ayurveda for Modern Wellness

Sanadana Herbals is a Kerala-based Ayurvedic and herbal wellness brand focused on safe, effective solutions—from herbal cough care to Ayurvedic support for piles—using time-tested formulations. Every batch is crafted in GMP-certified facilities for purity, consistency, and long-term wellness you can trust.

    Formulations guided by classical Ayurvedic principles, adapted thoughtfully for today’s lifestyle and digestive, respiratory, and circulatory care needs.

  • Natural & herbal ingredients

    We emphasize plant-based ingredients and holistic support—including care paths many people explore alongside guidance for concerns like piles—without relying on harsh chemicals.

  • GMP-certified manufacturing

    Structured processes, documented quality checks, and clean manufacturing environments so each product meets the same high standard, bottle after bottle.
